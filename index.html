Three steps to a research mind

01 — DISCOVER

Find the top labs

Search any research field. We use Claude with web search to rank the most influential researchers and surface who's doing the most important work.

02 — DISTILL

Extract the persona

We pull their papers from Semantic Scholar, search for talks, interviews, and lab websites. Claude synthesizes everything into a structured persona with a confidence report.

03 — CONVERSE

Think together

Have a real intellectual conversation with the distilled persona. Or assemble a roundtable — multiple researchers debating your idea in four different discussion modes.
