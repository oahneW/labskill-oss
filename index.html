Three steps to a research mind

01 — DISCOVER

Find the top labs

Search any field. We rank the most influential researchers and surface who is doing the most important work right now.

02 — DISTILL

Extract the mind

We pull papers from Semantic Scholar, bioRxiv, PubMed, and auto-search public talks, interviews, and lab websites. Claude synthesizes everything into a Skill with an Agentic Protocol — a search strategy derived from this researcher's epistemic standards.
